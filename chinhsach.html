Delivery area

Flower Corner Shop provides fresh flower ordering and flower delivery services in Ho Chi Minh City and 63 provinces and cities in Vietnam.

However, special delivery locations such as buildings with security blocking the delivery, conference centers...we will contact the recipient to arrange the most convenient flower delivery location for the recipient. receive.

Delivery time

Flower Corner commits to deliver flowers as soon as possible after 2 hours from the time the customer orders flowers and makes a successful payment. Delivery time is from 7:30 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. Orders placed after 8pm will be delivered the next morning.

However, in some cases such as rainstorm or uncontactable recipient, we will contact the orderer to arrange the earliest delivery at a different time.

Delivery charges

Flower Corner is free to deliver flowers within the inner city of Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi. The fee for flower delivery in the suburbs of Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi is 35,000 VND.

The fee for delivering flowers to other provinces and cities across the country is VND 60,000

***Free flower delivery program within Ho Chi Minh City will not be applied to orders that choose COD payment method
